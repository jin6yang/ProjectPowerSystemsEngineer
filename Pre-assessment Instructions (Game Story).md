## 2. Core Technical Indicators

As an engineer, you must develop muscle memory for the following two values:

- **P (Power, MW)**: This is the lifeblood of the power grid. Ensure that output always meets the terminal demand, and strictly avoid exceeding the **Load Capacity** of any component. Once overloaded, the system's power-off protection mechanism will forcibly shut down the current circuit.
- **S (Stability, STB)**: This is the skeleton of the power grid. Terminal equipment (such as AI Cores) is extremely sensitive to voltage fluctuations. If the STB falls below the equipment's requirement, the terminal will fail to start even if the power is sufficient.

## 3. Hardware Operational Standards

Please strictly adhere to these operational standards:

- **Energy Sources**: It is **strictly prohibited** to connect Micro Fusion Reactors (Orange) and Solar Panel Arrays (Blue) directly to one another. Energy sources must perform phase alignment (intermediary transfer) via transmission or storage buildings.
- **Transmission Loss**: The effective connection distance of Standard Cables is limited to **4 grids**. For long-distance transmission, you must install Relay Towers or Stabilizers.
- **Distributed Storage (BESS)**: Multiple adjacent green storage modules will automatically integrate into the grid to form a large resource pool. This is your only means of addressing peak-shaving demands (Max Discharge Time: 4S).

## 4. Terminal Security Protocols

The target buildings in the assessment (such as the **AI Core - Hub**) are valuable digital assets of the company:

- **Immovability**: Due to deep coupling between the terminals and the physical foundation, you **cannot move or remove** these buildings.
- **Extreme Stability Requirements**: Some AI Cores require an STB of **6** or higher. It is recommended to prioritize the stacking characteristics of **BESS (Green)** to boost stability.

## 5. Emergencies and Debugging

- **System Maintenance**: In rare cases, the system may simulate sudden failures to test the engineer's ability to work under pressure.
- **Forced Termination**: If it is determined that the current layout has completely deviated from the optimal solution, press and hold the `~` key for 3 seconds to manually apply for "Assessment Failure" and reboot the system.
